Monotone is a browser extension that reduces visual clutter on the web.


We constantly absorb visual input through the web, and multiple visual elements are always competing for our attention. Often, this visual input can distract from the core content of a webpage and make it difficult to decide what to focus on next.

This browser extension puts you in control of the visual noise you experience on the web by allowing you to choose a single color for all elements of a page. By simplifying the visual experience, you can focus on the parts of a webpage that matter the most to you.

You can choose between greyscale or five different monotone colors.
